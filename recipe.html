Recipe #1

    Mac + Cheese

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person

    • Recipe #2

    Ramen

  • Time: 15 mintues
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity #1

Step 1: Pour water into pot.

Step 2: Place pot over stove that is on.

Step 3: Let water boil, than add a bit of butter.

Step 4: Add macaroni in butter boiling water.

Step 5: Once macaroni is soft, drain all water.

Step 6: Add a little bit of milk in pot and stir.

Step 7: Add the packet of cheese that came in the box and stir.

Step 8: Grab plates/bowls and serve.

Source: Some recipes websites

Ingredients Quantity #2

Step 1: Pour water into pot.

Step 2: Wait till water boils in pot over stove.

Step 3: Once water is boiling, insert packen ramen in water.(without the wrapper)

Step 4: Wait about 2 minutes, than add seasoning.

Step 5: Stir until ramen is soft, but not to soft.

Step 6: Once ready serve noodles with a bit of the ramen juice.
